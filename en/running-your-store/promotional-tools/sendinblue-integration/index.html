    What Sendinblue is

    Sendinblue is an all-in-one digital marketing toolbox capable of taking care of the entirety of your marketing needs. Sendinblue empowers to build and grow relationships using email campaigns, SMS messages, and sophisticated automation of marketing workflows.

    The Sendinblue integration for nopCommerce allows a store owner to:

    • design clear and professional newsletters
    • build and target the audience
    • automate the marketing (for example, abandoned cart recovery)
